## Who we are

Roost is a shared household app for couples, built by Ember Studio. For the purposes of UK and EU data protection law, Ember Studio is the **data controller** — meaning we decide what personal data is collected and why.

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email us at **thomasslatergg@gmail.com**.

---

## What data we collect and why

### Account information
**What:** Your email address, name, and (if you sign in with Google) your Google profile name and profile picture URL.
**Why:** To create and identify your account, and to let your partner see who you are in the app.
**Legal basis:** Performance of contract — this data is necessary to provide the service.

### Profile data
**What:** The display name and avatar (colour and icon) you choose within the app.
**Why:** To personalise your experience and help your partner identify you throughout the app.
**Legal basis:** Performance of contract.

### Household content
**What:** Everything you and your partner create inside the app — shopping list items, expense records and amounts, chore names and descriptions, budget limits, calendar events, activity feed entries, and notes.
**Why:** To provide the core features of Roost: a shared, real-time view of your household.
**Legal basis:** Performance of contract.

### Preferences and settings
**What:** Your notification preferences, display preferences (week start day, time format, date format, currency), and theme choice.
**Why:** To remember your settings between sessions.
**Legal basis:** Performance of contract.

### Technical data
**What:** Basic information about how you use the app (for example, when you log in or when a sync event occurs). We do **not** use any analytics tools, advertising trackers, or crash reporting services.
**Why:** To keep the service working correctly.
**Legal basis:** Legitimate interests — maintaining a functioning service.

## How we use AI (Hazel)

Roost includes an AI assistant called **Hazel** that silently tidies up text you enter — for example, normalising a shopping item name or categorising an expense. To do this, short text snippets are sent to **Anthropic's Claude API**.

**What is sent:** Only the text of the specific item being processed (e.g. "2x oat milk", "Uber to airport"). No account information, names, or other personal details are attached.
**Who receives it:** Anthropic, PBC — a US company. See "International transfers" below.
**Anthropic's data use:** Anthropic does not use API inputs to train its models.
**Legal basis:** Legitimate interests — Hazel provides a meaningful improvement to the experience and processes only the minimum text necessary.

You can read Anthropic's privacy policy at [anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).

## International transfers

Supabase and Anthropic are based in the United States. Transferring personal data from the UK or EU to the US requires appropriate safeguards under UK GDPR / EU GDPR. Both Supabase and Anthropic operate under **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)**, which are the approved legal mechanism for these transfers.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Restriction** — ask us to pause processing of your data in certain circumstances
- **Objection** — object to processing based on legitimate interests

Most of these you can exercise directly within the app (Settings → Account). For anything else, email us at **thomasslatergg@gmail.com** and we will respond within 30 days.

**UK residents:** You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)** at [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk) or by calling 0303 123 1113.

**EU residents:** You may lodge a complaint with the data protection authority in your country of residence.
